Boundary Surveys

Residential & Commercial

A boundary survey establishes the legal corners and lines of a property based on the recorded deed, plat, and adjoining surveys. Whether you're buying land, building a fence, resolving a neighbor dispute, or preparing for construction, an accurate boundary survey is the foundation of every informed decision.

Our boundary surveys are performed by a Licensed Professional Land Surveyor in accordance with Texas state standards. We research deed records, analyze the chain of title, locate physical monuments, and prepare a signed and sealed plat of survey as a legal document.

Typical Deliverables

  • Signed and sealed survey plat
  • Legal description of property
  • Monument placement at corners
  • Deed and title research
  • Digital file (DWG/PDF)
02

Topographic Surveys

Site Design & Engineering

Topographic surveys capture the three-dimensional shape of a site — elevations, contours, natural features, and existing improvements. Engineers, architects, and developers rely on topo surveys to design grading plans, drainage systems, building pads, and utility layouts.

We collect field data using RTK GPS and robotic total stations, supplemented by UAV photogrammetry on larger sites. Deliverables are compatible with AutoCAD Civil 3D and ArcGIS for seamless integration with your design workflow.

Typical Deliverables

  • Contour map at specified intervals
  • Spot elevations across site
  • Existing improvements and features
  • Digital terrain model (DTM)
  • AutoCAD Civil 3D & PDF deliverables
03

Construction Layout & Staking

Construction Support

Construction staking translates the engineer's plans into physical locations on the ground. We stake building corners, foundation lines, road centerlines, utility alignments, grading limits, and any other feature that the contractor needs to build to plan.

Accurate staking prevents costly construction errors and change orders. We work from your civil plans, coordinate with project managers, and adapt to phased construction schedules to keep work moving.

Typical Deliverables

  • Building corner and offset stakes
  • Road centerline and slope stakes
  • Utility alignment staking
  • Grading and finish floor elevations
  • As-built verification
04

ALTA/NSPS Land Title Surveys

Commercial Real Estate

ALTA/NSPS surveys are the national standard for commercial real estate transactions. Required by lenders, title companies, and attorneys, these surveys document property boundaries, improvements, encumbrances, easements, and encroachments at a level of detail that protects all parties in a commercial transaction.

Our ALTA surveys comply with the current Minimum Standard Detail Requirements and include all standard Table A items. We coordinate directly with title companies and legal counsel to ensure timely delivery on transaction deadlines.

Typical Deliverables

  • Signed and sealed ALTA plat
  • Title commitment review and response
  • All Table A items as selected
  • Zoning classification documentation
  • Flood zone determination
  • Digital DWG and PDF files
05

Subdivision & Platting

Land Development

Subdivision and platting services support land developers and property owners through the process of dividing a tract into legally recorded lots. This involves preparing the subdivision plat, coordinating with city and county planning departments, and navigating the regulatory approval process from first submission to recorded instrument.

We handle replats, minor plats, amending plats, and full subdivision plats. Our experience with local municipalities and county regulations means fewer re-submittals and faster approval timelines.

Typical Deliverables

  • Preliminary and final subdivision plat
  • Legal descriptions for each lot
  • Regulatory submittal preparation
  • Coordination with planning department
  • Recorded plat instrument
