About Southline

Five years of precision-driven surveying and geospatial services, built on a foundation of accuracy, technology, and direct professional service.

Our Background

Built for Accuracy.
Designed for the Field.

Southline Surveying & Mapping was founded five years ago with one guiding principle: deliver precise, reliable survey work with the responsiveness and transparency that clients actually need. We serve homeowners, engineers, contractors, developers, and public agencies across the Southern United States with precision-driven solutions.

Our approach combines traditional surveying principles — the craft of understanding boundaries, elevations, and legal descriptions — with cutting-edge geospatial technology including RTK/PPK GPS systems, robotic total stations, and UAV-based LiDAR platforms. This combination allows us to tackle projects from a single residential lot to large-scale corridor mapping with equal accuracy and professionalism.

As a small, focused firm, every project is handled by a licensed professional. You won't be handed off to a junior technician or left waiting on callbacks. We communicate clearly, respond quickly, and deliver work that stands up to review.
