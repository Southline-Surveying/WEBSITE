What We Offer

Advanced Geospatial Services

We deliver actionable spatial data with the accuracy, efficiency, and technical precision that engineering and infrastructure projects demand.

LiDAR Services

Airborne and terrestrial LiDAR data acquisition for terrain modeling, infrastructure mapping, and volumetric analysis at any scale.

  • UAV LiDAR data acquisition
  • Point cloud processing & classification
  • Digital Terrain Models (DTM)
  • Digital Surface Models (DSM)
  • Contour generation at any interval
  • Earthwork volume calculations
  • Corridor and infrastructure mapping

Applications: Infrastructure, utilities, floodplain analysis, large-area terrain modeling.

UAV Aerial Mapping

High-resolution photogrammetric data collection using FAA Part 107 certified drone operations for mapping, inspection, and documentation.

  • Orthomosaic imagery
  • High-resolution aerial photography
  • 3D point cloud from photogrammetry
  • Construction progress documentation
  • Stockpile volume measurement
  • Large-site mapping
  • Inspection imagery & reporting

Applications: Construction management, aggregate stockpiles, large parcel documentation.

GIS & Remote Sensing

Spatial data integration, analysis, and management for environmental, municipal, and infrastructure asset applications.

  • GIS data integration & management
  • Raster & vector data analysis
  • Terrain modeling & analysis
  • Change detection
  • Environmental mapping
  • Asset mapping & inventory

Compatible with: AutoCAD Civil 3D, ArcGIS, MicroStation, QGIS, and major GIS platforms.
